## System Requirements

The following are the minimal [recommended requirements](https://www.kaspersky.com/):

* **RAM**: 4 GB or more
* **CPU**: 2 cores or higher
* **Disk**: at least 1 GB of free space
* **bandwidth**: more fluent experience over 100M
