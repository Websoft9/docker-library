## System Requirements

The following are the minimal [recommended requirements](https://www.onyx.app/):

* **RAM**: 10 GB or more
* **CPU**: 4 cores or higher
* **Disk**: at least 50 GB of free space
* **bandwidth**: more fluent experience over 100M
